# Bloomport Content Calendar: 6-Month Plan
**Period:** May 26, 2026 – November 30, 2026  
**Cadence:** 2 blog posts/week + 1 tool page/month  
**Total Content Pieces:** ~54 articles + 6 tool pages = 60 pieces  
**Platform:** bloomport.fun/blog

---

## Strategy Overview

| Phase | Months | Focus | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| Foundation | May–June | Easy keywords, cluster 1 & 4 quick wins | Establish topical authority, first rankings |
| Growth | July–August | Cluster 2 expansion, seasonal hooks | Scale traffic, improve domain authority |
| Monetization | September–November | Cluster 3 (AI tools), comparison content | Maximize RPM, drive app conversions |

**Publishing Rule:** Never publish two articles in the same keyword cluster back-to-back. Alternate clusters to signal broad topical relevance.

## Content Quality Checklist (Run Before Every Publish)

- [ ] Keyword appears in H1, within first 100 words, and in at least 2 H2s
- [ ] Meta title is 50-60 characters
- [ ] Meta description is 145-155 characters
- [ ] At least 3 internal links (2 to related articles + 1 to /app)
- [ ] Bloomport CTA appears in body (not just footer)
- [ ] Article includes a tool, checklist, prompt list, or interactive element
- [ ] JSON-LD schema added (Article or HowTo)
- [ ] Image alt tags filled out with keyword context
- [ ] Word count meets target (1,200 min for tool pages, 1,600+ for blogs)
