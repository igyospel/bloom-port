## 5. HARO/Connectively Strategy

Responding to queries on HARO (Help a Reporter Out) / Connectively secures high-quality editorial links from major publications like Forbes, Healthline, and HuffPost.

### Bloomport Expert Bio
*Under 100 words. Ready to copy-paste into submissions.*

> **Bio:** [Name] is a mental wellness technologist and content strategist at Bloomport (bloomport.fun) — a free AI mindful productivity platform. With a background in cognitive psychology and digital wellness, they focus on building accessible tools for stress reduction, habit tracking, and focus. They can speak authoritatively on mindful productivity, tech-enabled wellness, chronic overthinking, and building sustainable work routines.

### 5 Topic Categories to Monitor
1. **Mental Health & Wellbeing** (anxiety, chronic overthinking, mindfulness techniques)
2. **Productivity & Work-from-Home** (Pomodoro technique, burnout prevention, task management)
3. **Daily Habits & Routines** (morning routines, sleep improvement, habit formation)
4. **Technology & Wellness** (AI for health, digital detox, screen time limits)
5. **Business/Entrepreneur Stress** (managing executive fatigue, decision-making anxiety)
